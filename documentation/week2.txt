Week 2: Data understanding and initial data exploration.

In the second week, the selected dataset was explored in detail. The dataset structure was analyzed to understand the different columns, data types, and number of records.

Key attributes such as ticket priority, issue category, resolution time, and ticket status were examined. Initial observations were made to understand patterns and trends within the dataset.

This step helped in identifying important variables that would be useful for analyzing IT support team performance. The main objective of this week was to gain a clear understanding of the dataset before performing data cleaning and transformation.
