WEEK 6 – Data Transformation & Power Query

In Week 6, we focused on data cleaning and transformation using Power Query. We worked on handling missing values, removing duplicates, correcting data types, and standardizing the dataset. We also explored column quality, column distribution, and column profiling to understand the data better. The ETL (Extract, Transform, Load) process was introduced, where data is extracted from sources, transformed in Power Query, and then loaded into Power BI. This step ensured that the dataset is clean and ready for further analysis and visualization.
