WEEK 7 – DAX & Measures

In Week 7, we learned about DAX (Data Analysis Expressions), which is used to create calculated measures in Power BI. We understood the difference between implicit and explicit measures and learned how to create custom measures using functions like SUM, AVERAGE, COUNT, and CALCULATE. The concept of filter context was introduced, and we learned how CALCULATE modifies filters to perform conditional analysis. We also created business-related metrics such as total tickets, average resolution time, and CSAT score. This helped in performing dynamic and real-time analysis.
